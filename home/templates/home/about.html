Embarr's Mission Statement

About Us:

We are a family run retirement home for rescued aged or abused horses that can no longer be ridden. We offer a safe haven for these sentient creatures. We are a registered charity and we need your donations and support. We do not rehome our horses, they are with us until the end. If you want to volunteer to help out please give us a call or email. If you have a welfare concern for horses in your area, you can submit an annonymous report - see link at the base of the page.

Report a Welfare Concern

If you have any concerns about the welfare of a horse or any other creature, please feel comfortable submitting you concerns anonymously in the form below and we will take discreet measures to investigate and take action if appropriate.
